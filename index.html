About Us

The story behind the lens

At Memories by Abhi, we believe every tradition tells a story — and every story deserves to be told with beauty, respect, and artistry. Specializing in Indian traditional ceremonies, we bring a deep cultural understanding and cinematic eye to every event we capture.

From the vibrant colors of a South Indian wedding to the intimate warmth of a griha pravesh, from the joyful celebration of a half saree ceremony to grand receptions — we are there to preserve your most precious moments so you can relive them for generations.
